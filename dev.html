Game Details:

The game is designed to challenge players in a survival situation, navigating from a plane crash to safety.

Players must explore the Welsh wilderness, gather resources, solve environmental puzzles, and make critical decisions that affect their journey.

Along the way, they will encounter dynamic weather, wildlife, and unexpected obstacles that test their problem-solving and adaptability.

The game emphasizes teamwork, resource management, and strategic planning, offering multiple paths and endings based on player choices.

Immersive visuals and an engaging storyline aim to provide a unique and educational adventure experience.
